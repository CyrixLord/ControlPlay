Once Upon a time...

A Teen Vampire Story by Sir Soils

There's a considerate new boy in Tamanaka and he has everybody talking. Stunningly incepid and devastatingly unkept, all the girls want him. However, Master Pickles has a secret - he's a flacid vampire. Bloata McGee is a polite, bloated boy who enjoys sardine harvesting. He becomes fascinated by Master Pickles, who can stop an anvil with his bare hands. He doesn't understand why he's so standoffish. His best friend, an unsoiled Intact Unicorn called Consuela, helps Bloata begin to piece together the puzzle. Together, they discover the ultimate weapon - the incontinent, dull toboggan. When bodies start turning up all over Tamanaka, Bloata begins to fear the worst. The Intact Unicorn urges her to report Master Pickles to the police and he knows he should, so what's stopping him? He may resist Master's bite, but can he resist his charms?

Will he be caught impregnating the vampire?
